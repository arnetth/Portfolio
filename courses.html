craigslist Homepage Redesign

Designed by me after secondary research, conducting a heuristic evaluation of the original homepage, and creating a moodboard and style guide. A/B testing with participants was performed in comparison to the original craigslist homepage and resulted in more positive associations towards this redesign.

Project Management and Video Editing

"Intersection of Cultures"

I partook in a virtual creative and culture exchange program where Purdue University students collaborated with students from ITM and Universidad de Antioquia of Colombia.

"Intersection of Cultures" is an immersive map of an imaginary town that is a combination of Medellin, Colombia and West Lafayette, IN, US. I worked together with students from both Purdue and ITM to create this project to be displayed in a Purdue art exhibit.

I managed the project from start to finish and edited the videos using Adobe Premiere Pro. My original idea was to create an interactive map where exhibit visitors could use a hand controller to personally move around the map and explore it, but because of COVID-19 restrictions, we could only use a video displayed on a projector.
